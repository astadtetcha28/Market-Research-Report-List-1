<p><p>The tachogenerator market is primarily categorized into DC and AC tachogenerators. DC tachogenerators produce a direct current output proportional to the rotational speed of a shaft, known for their simplicity and reliability, commonly used in precision speed control applications. In contrast, AC tachogenerators generate an alternating current output, typically favored for higher performance in dynamic environments, offering better efficiency at varying speeds. Both types are essential in various industries, including manufacturing and automation, for precise speed measurement and control.</p></p>
