<p><p>The acetal plastics market is primarily divided into two types: Homo-polyoxymethylene (POM) and Co-polyoxymethylene (co-POM). Homo-POM is characterized by its high rigidity, strength, and chemical resistance, making it ideal for precision parts and automotive applications. In contrast, Co-POM is formulated to enhance impact resistance and thermal stability, offering better performance in challenging environments. Both types cater to diverse industries, including automotive, electronics, and consumer goods, due to their favorable mechanical properties and versatility in various applications.</p></p>
