<p><p>The Digital Dental X-Ray Sensors market includes intraoral and extraoral X-rays, each serving distinct diagnostic purposes. Intraoral X-rays involve sensors placed inside the mouth, capturing detailed images of individual teeth and surrounding structures, essential for detecting cavities, root issues, and treatment planning. Extraoral X-rays, on the other hand, are taken outside the mouth, providing broader images of the jaw, teeth, and facial structure. Both types enhance diagnostic accuracy, improve patient comfort, and streamline dental care efficiency.</p></p>
