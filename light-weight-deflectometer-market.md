<p><p>The Light Weight Deflectometer (LWD) is an essential tool used in civil engineering to assess the load-bearing capacity and structural integrity of pavement and soil. It operates by dropping a known weight from a predetermined height onto a test plate, measuring the deflection to evaluate the material's stiffness. The LWD is valued for its portability, ease of operation, and its ability to provide immediate results, making it ideal for on-site assessments.</p><p>The Light Weight Deflectometer Market is projected to grow at a CAGR of 4.2% during the forecast period.
