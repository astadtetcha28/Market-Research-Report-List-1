<p><p>The pneumatic cylinders market is primarily categorized into single-acting and double-acting cylinders, along with other specialized types. Single-acting cylinders utilize air pressure to move the piston in one direction, returning it via a spring or external force, making them ideal for applications requiring minimal force. In contrast, double-acting cylinders use air pressure for both extension and retraction, offering more power and control for demanding tasks. Additionally, the "others" category encompasses niche cylinders used in unique or specialized applications.</p></p>
