<p><p>Electric hydraulic pumps are vital in various industrial applications, categorized mainly into piston and positive-displacement types. Piston pumps utilize reciprocating pistons to convert electric energy into hydraulic pressure, offering high efficiency and performance for demanding applications. Positive-displacement pumps, on the other hand, move a fixed volume of fluid with each cycle, ensuring reliable flow under varying pressures. Both types cater to different operational needs, emphasizing performance, efficiency, and precision in fluid management.</p></p>
