<p><p>Physical Vapor Deposition (PVD) on plastics involves coating plastic substrates with thin films to enhance their properties. The main types include deposition, which vaporizes materials to form a coating; sputtering, where energetic particles dislodge atoms from a target to deposit on the plastic; and pulsed laser deposition, which uses focused laser pulses to vaporize materials and deposit them onto the surface. These techniques improve durability, aesthetics, and functionality, making them valuable in various industries, including electronics and automotive.</p></p>
