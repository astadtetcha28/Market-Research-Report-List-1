<p><ul><li>Manometric Methods</li><li>Colorimetric Methods</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Portable BOD Analyzer Market encompasses two primary types: manometric and colorimetric methods. Manometric methods measure changes in gas pressure resulting from biochemical oxygen demand (BOD) consumption, providing real-time results in various environments. Colorimetric methods, on the other hand, rely on chemical reactions that produce color changes in a sample, allowing quantification of BOD levels through optical measurements. Both methods cater to diverse applications, such as wastewater treatment and environmental monitoring, enhancing efficiency and accuracy in assessing water quality.</p></p>
