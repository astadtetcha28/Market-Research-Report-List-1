<p><ul><li>Physical Cell Disruption Method</li><li>Reagent Based Cell Disruption Method</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The cell disruption method market is divided into two primary types: physical and reagent-based methods. Physical cell disruption involves mechanical techniques, such as high-pressure homogenization or sonication, to break cell walls and release intracellular contents. In contrast, reagent-based methods utilize chemicals, enzymes, or systems like detergents to lyse cells, often offering gentler solutions for sensitive cell types. Each method has unique applications, advantages, and limitations, catering to varying research and industrial needs in biotechnology and pharmaceuticals.</p></p>
