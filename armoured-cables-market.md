<p><ul><li>Aluminium Wire Armour</li><li>Steel Wire Armour</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The armoured cables market consists primarily of two types: Aluminium Wire Armour (AWA) and Steel Wire Armour (SWA). AWA features lightweight aluminum conductors, offering excellent corrosion resistance and suitability for less demanding environments, often employed in indoor applications. Conversely, SWA utilizes robust steel layers, providing high mechanical protection and durability, making it ideal for outdoor installations and heavy-duty uses like industrial settings. Both serve to enhance cable safety and reliability in various electrical applications.</p></p>
