<p><p>The vacuum drying oven market is categorized into two primary types: vertical and horizontal. Vertical vacuum drying ovens utilize a vertical design, which allows for efficient space utilization and is ideal for drying bulkier materials. In contrast, horizontal vacuum drying ovens offer a more accessible layout, facilitating easy loading and unloading of products. Both types effectively remove moisture at low temperatures, preserving the integrity of the materials, but are selected based on specific application needs and available workspace.</p></p>
