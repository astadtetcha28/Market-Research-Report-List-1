<p><strong>The Woodworking Fasteners Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Threaded Fasteners</li><li>Non-threaded Fasteners</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The woodworking fasteners market is divided into two main types: threaded fasteners and non-threaded fasteners. Threaded fasteners, such as screws and bolts, feature helical ridges that provide strong mechanical connections, making them ideal for securing joints and structures in woodworking projects. In contrast, non-threaded fasteners, like nails, staples, and dowels, rely on friction and adhesion for stability, offering quick assembly and ease of use. Both types play crucial roles in ensuring the durability and integrity of wood constructions.</p></p>
