<p><p>The farm tractor market is primarily divided into two types: wheel tractors and crawler tractors. Wheel tractors are versatile and widely used for various farming tasks on flat terrain, offering high speed and maneuverability. They are suitable for tasks like plowing, tilling, and harvesting. In contrast, crawler tractors, equipped with tracked systems, excel in rough or uneven terrains, providing better traction and stability. Their robust design makes them ideal for heavy-duty tasks such as land clearing and hauling. Both types play crucial roles in modern agriculture.</p></p>
