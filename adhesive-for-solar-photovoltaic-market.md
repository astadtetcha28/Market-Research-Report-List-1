<p><p>The adhesive for the solar photovoltaic market is categorized into three main types: epoxy adhesive, silicone adhesive, and others. Epoxy adhesives provide strong bonds and durability, making them ideal for structural applications in solar panels. Silicone adhesives offer flexibility and weather resistance, essential for outdoor applications where thermal expansion occurs. Other adhesives may include polyurethane and acrylic options, each catering to specific needs such as bonding transparent materials or enhancing environmental resistance in various photovoltaic installations.</p></p>
