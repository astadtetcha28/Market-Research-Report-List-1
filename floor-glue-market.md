<p><p>The floor glue market encompasses various types, each catering to specific flooring needs. Epoxy adhesives are known for their strong bonding and durability, making them ideal for heavy traffic areas. Polyurethane adhesives offer flexibility and resistance to moisture, suitable for dynamic environments. Acrylic adhesives provide quick drying times and versatility, often used in residential applications. Vinyl adhesives are designed specifically for vinyl flooring, ensuring secure adhesion while accommodating expansion and contraction. Each type serves distinct applications based on performance and environmental conditions.</p></p>
