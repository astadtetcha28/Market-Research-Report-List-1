<p><p>CAD/CAM milling machines in the dental laboratory market utilize both subtractive and additive processes for tooth restoration and prosthetic fabrication. Subtractive processes involve removing material from a dental block to create precise crowns, bridges, and other restorations, ensuring high accuracy and detail. In contrast, additive processes build up structures layer by layer, enabling the creation of complex geometries and customized solutions. Together, these technologies enhance efficiency, precision, and customization in dental manufacturing, catering to diverse patient needs.</p></p>
