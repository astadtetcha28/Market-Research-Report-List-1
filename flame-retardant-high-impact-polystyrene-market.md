<p><p>The Flame Retardant High Impact Polystyrene (HIPS) market is categorized into two main types: Halogen Type and Halogen Free Type. Halogen Type HIPS incorporates bromine or chlorine-based flame retardants, providing effective fire resistance but raising environmental and health concerns. Conversely, Halogen Free Type HIPS employs alternative flame retardants, which are considered safer and more environmentally friendly, making them increasingly popular in various applications. Both types are utilized in industries such as automotive, electronics, and construction for their durability and resistance to heat.</p></p>
