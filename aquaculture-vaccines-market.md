<p><p>The aquaculture vaccines market encompasses three primary types: oral, injectable, and immersion vaccines. Oral vaccines are administered via feed, fostering ease of use and higher acceptance among fish. Injectable vaccines are delivered through direct injection, ensuring precise dosage and prompt immune response, but may require skilled handling. Immersion vaccines involve immersing fish in a vaccine solution, allowing for mass vaccination in environments where traditional methods are impractical. Each type plays a vital role in enhancing fish health and managing disease outbreaks in aquaculture.</p></p>
