<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Medical Occluder Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>PDA Occluder</li><li>ASD Occluder</li><li>VSD Occluder</li><li>LAA Occluder</li><li>PFO Occluder</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The medical occluder market comprises various types designed for specific cardiac conditions. PDA occluders treat patent ductus arteriosus, while ASD occluders address atrial septal defects. VSD occluders are used for ventricular septal defects, and LAA occluders target left atrial appendage closure to reduce stroke risk in atrial fibrillation patients. PFO occluders effectively close patent foramen ovale to prevent potential embolic events. Each device serves a distinct purpose in managing congenital or acquired heart conditions, enhancing patient outcomes.</p></p>
