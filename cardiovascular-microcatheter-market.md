<p><ul><li>Single Lumen</li><li>Dual Lumen</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The cardiovascular microcatheter market is primarily segmented into single lumen and dual lumen types. Single lumen microcatheters are designed for straightforward access to vascular pathways, allowing a single guidewire or instrument to pass through, making them suitable for specific interventional procedures. In contrast, dual lumen microcatheters feature two channels, enabling simultaneous access for both delivery and aspiration, which enhances procedural efficiency and flexibility. These varying designs cater to different therapeutic needs in cardiovascular interventions, influencing market dynamics and demand.</p></p>
