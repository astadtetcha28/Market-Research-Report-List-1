<p><strong>The Business Ethernet Service Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>FTTC</li><li>FTTP</li><li>EFM</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Business Ethernet Service market includes several types of connectivity solutions. FTTC (Fiber to the Cabinet) delivers high-speed internet by connecting fiber optics to a street cabinet, then using copper for the final link to businesses. FTTP (Fiber to the Premises) provides a direct fiber connection to the business location, ensuring faster speeds and reliability. EFM (Ethernet in the First Mile) utilizes copper pairs to provide Ethernet services over existing infrastructure, offering flexibility and cost-effective solutions for enterprises.</p></p>
