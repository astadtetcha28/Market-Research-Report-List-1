<p><p>The insect-based fertilizer market encompasses various types, including ants, crickets, silkworms, cicadas, mealworms, and grasshoppers, among others. Together, these insect sources represent an eco-friendly, sustainable alternative to conventional fertilizers, promoting agricultural productivity.</p></p>
