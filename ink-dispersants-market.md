<p><p>The ink dispersants market can be categorized into two main types: water-based and solvent-based dispersants. Water-based dispersants are eco-friendly, offering low volatility and reduced solvent emissions, making them ideal for industries focused on sustainability. In contrast, solvent-based dispersants provide enhanced performance in terms of pigment stabilization and compatibility with various inks, often resulting in vivid colors and better flow properties. Each type serves specific applications, catering to diverse printing technologies and end-user requirements.</p></p>
