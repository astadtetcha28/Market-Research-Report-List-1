<p><p>The tea drying machine market includes two primary types: tea frying dryers and tea baking dryers. Tea frying dryers utilize high temperatures for rapid moisture removal, enhancing flavor and aroma, commonly used for green teas. In contrast, tea baking dryers apply gentler heat over a longer duration, which is ideal for black and oolong teas, preserving delicate flavors and aromas. Both types are essential for producing high-quality tea by ensuring optimal drying conditions tailored to specific tea varieties.</p></p>
