<p><strong>The Temporary External Pacemaker Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Chamber Temporary Pacemaker</li><li>Dual Chamber Temporary Pacemaker</li><li>Triple Chamber Temporary Pacemaker</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The temporary external pacemaker market is categorized into three types: single chamber, dual chamber, and triple chamber pacemakers. Single chamber temporary pacemakers stimulate one heart chamber, typically used for simple pacing needs. Dual chamber pacemakers coordinate pacing in both the atrium and ventricle, enhancing cardiac efficiency. Triple chamber pacemakers, also known as cardiac resynchronization therapy devices, target all three chambers to improve heart function in heart failure patients. Each type serves specific clinical requirements based on patient conditions.</p></p>
