<p><p>The aluminum foil market is categorized into three main types based on thickness: heavy gauge foil, medium gauge foil, and light gauge foil. Heavy gauge foil, typically thicker than 0.009 inches, is used for industrial applications and packaging requiring durability. Medium gauge foil ranges between 0.004 to 0.009 inches and is commonly utilized in food service and preservation. Light gauge foil, thinner than 0.004 inches, is primarily used for household purposes, such as cooking and wrapping, due to its flexibility and ease of use.</p></p>
