<p><ul><li>Combustion-Type</li><li>Electric-Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The smelting furnaces market is categorized into combustion-type and electric-type furnaces. Combustion-type smelting furnaces utilize fossil fuels, such as coal or natural gas, to generate heat for metal extraction. They are typically favored for their high capacity and efficiency in large-scale operations. In contrast, electric-type smelting furnaces use electrical energy, providing more precise temperature control and reduced emissions. While electric-type furnaces are often used for specialized applications, combustion-types remain prevalent in traditional metal production due to their cost-effectiveness.</p></p>
