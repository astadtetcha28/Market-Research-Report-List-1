<p><p>The security turnstile market is divided primarily into two categories: optical turnstiles and arm turnstiles. Optical turnstiles utilize infrared sensors to detect movement, allowing for smooth, quick access while maintaining high security levels. These turnstiles are sleek and often used in modern facilities. In contrast, arm turnstiles feature physical barriers that restrict access until an authorized signal is received, providing robust security. Both types cater to various safety needs, enhancing entry control in commercial and public spaces.</p></p>
