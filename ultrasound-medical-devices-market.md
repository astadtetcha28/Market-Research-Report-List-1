<p><p>The ultrasound medical devices market comprises two main types: diagnostic and therapeutic ultrasound. Diagnostic ultrasound utilizes high-frequency sound waves to create images of organs and tissues, aiding in the identification of medical conditions. Common applications include obstetrics, cardiology, and internal medicine. Conversely, therapeutic ultrasound employs sound waves for treatment purposes, such as breaking down kidney stones or promoting tissue healing. Both sectors leverage advanced technology to enhance patient care, contributing significantly to the medical imaging and treatment landscape.</p></p>
