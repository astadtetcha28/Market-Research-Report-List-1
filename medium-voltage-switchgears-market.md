<p><strong>The Medium Voltage Switchgears Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Air Insulated Switchgears</li><li>Gas Insulated Switchgears</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The medium voltage switchgear market comprises three main types: Air Insulated Switchgears (AIS), Gas Insulated Switchgears (GIS), and others. AIS utilizes air as an insulating medium and is well-suited for outdoor applications, offering a cost-effective solution. GIS, on the other hand, employs sulfur hexafluoride gas for insulation, making it compact and ideal for urban settings with limited space. The "Others" category includes hybrid solutions and modular switchgear configurations, catering to diverse applications and specific customer requirements.</p></p>
