<p><p>The Superoxide Dismutase (SOD) market is segmented into three main types: Cu.Zn-SOD, Mn-SOD, and Fe-SOD. Cu.Zn-SOD is predominantly found in the cytoplasm of cells, playing a vital role in protecting against oxidative stress. Mn-SOD is located in the mitochondria and is essential for cellular respiration and energy production. Fe-SOD, mainly present in bacteria and some plants, contributes to detoxifying superoxide radicals. Each type serves distinct functions and applications in health, cosmetics, and agriculture, driving market differentiation.</p></p>
