<p><strong>The Subsea Pipeline Engineering Services Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>S-Lay Installation</li><li>J-Lay Installation</li><li>Drag-In Installation</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Subsea pipeline engineering services encompass various installation techniques tailored for underwater pipeline projects. S-Lay installation involves laying pipelines on the seabed in a lazy "S" configuration, suitable for relatively shallow waters. J-Lay installation, characterized by a vertical installation method, is ideal for deeper waters and offers greater control in challenging environments. Drag-in installation involves pulling a pre-installed pipeline segment into position, typically employed in connection with pre-laid structures. Each method is selected based on project depth, environmental conditions, and logistical considerations.</p></p>
