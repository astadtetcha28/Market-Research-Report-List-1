<p><p>The glass seal market is divided into low-temperature sealing glass and high-temperature sealing glass. Low-temperature sealing glass operates effectively at temperatures below 300°C, commonly used in applications like electronics and photovoltaics, where thermal stress must be minimized. In contrast, high-temperature sealing glass is designed for environments exceeding 300°C, suitable for industries like aerospace and automotive, where durability against extreme thermal conditions is crucial. Both types serve essential roles in enhancing the reliability and performance of various products and applications.</p></p>
